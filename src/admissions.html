Admissions

Admission Criteria

To apply for admission to the Computer Science Department, prospective students must meet the following requirements:

  • Minimum of 50% marks in intermediate or equivalent examination.
  • Applicants must have completed mathematics or computer science as a subject in their previous education.
  • Applicants must submit valid documentation including marksheets and identification proofs.

Application Steps

The application process is simple and straightforward. Follow the steps below to apply:

  1. Fill out the online application form available on the UCP website.
  2. Upload the required documents including transcripts and identity proof.
  3. Pay the application fee online or through bank transfer.
  4. Submit your application before the deadline.
  5. Wait for the interview call (if required) from the admissions office.

Deadlines

The following deadlines apply for the upcoming academic session:

  • Application Deadline: July 15, 2026
  • Last date to submit documents: July 20, 2026
  • Interview (if applicable): July 25-30, 2026

Degree Programs Offered

The Computer Science Department offers the following degree programs:

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS)
  • Master of Science in Computer Science (MSCS)
  • PhD in Computer Science (PhD CS)
